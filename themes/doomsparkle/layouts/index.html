The Seattle Filmmakers slack is a slack community of Seattle-based filmmakers who help each other on projects, share ideas, and generally support each other as a community. We are a community open to anyone who makes films and lives or works in the Seattle area.

All users of the Seattle Filmmakers slack are responsible for following the Code of Conduct.
